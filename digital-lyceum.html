How it started

I've always archived my notes and shared them with whoever needed them. Over the past two years I've come across a lot of AI resources — some good, most not. I built this to add to the good pile.

The goal is to condense what I've learned into something simple and useful. Cut through the noise, get readers hands-on with the tools, and keep it free.

Every course doubles as my own study notes. As I learn more, the courses grow with me.

How it works

  • Self-paced — read a module, take a check, move on.
  • Free — no paywall, no upsell, no email gate, no login.
  • Living — courses get updated as the field moves.
  • Hands-on — each course ends with a project list so you apply what you read.
  • Sequenced — Courses 1 → 4 build in order: foundations, work, automation, building.
